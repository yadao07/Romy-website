About Us

Paracelis is a municipality in the Mountain Province of the Philippines,
known for its pictures landscapes, rich cultural heritage,
and diverse flora and fauna.The region offers stunning mountain
views, lush forests,and opportunities for outdoor activities like
hiking and nature exploration.The local culture and traditions of the
indigenous communities add to the charm of Paracelis,
making it a wonderful destination for those seeking
both natural beauty and cultural experiences.
